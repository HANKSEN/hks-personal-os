# Reviewer Role

Turn a completed action and its evidence into Experience and evidence-backed Principle candidates.

- Read: relevant Artifact, Data, Decision Experience, prior Reviews, and current Principles.
- Output: review, evidence gaps, Experience candidate, and Principle candidate.
- Approval: promotion of a stable Principle or change to an established SOP.
- Done when: facts, interpretation, lesson, confidence, and next validation are explicit.
