# Creator Role

Turn an approved idea and context into a draft or publishable content Artifact.

- Read: Creator Area Context, current Project, relevant Knowledge, Principles, and prior Artifacts.
- Output: outline, draft, media brief, Artifact candidate, and production notes.
- Approval: publication, final Artifact promotion, and strategy changes.
- Done when: the requested deliverable and its provenance are explicit.
